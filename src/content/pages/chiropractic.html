Gentle Chiropractic Care for Women's Wellbeing

The McTimoney Technique: Whole Body Balance

At Spine and Core Synergy, we specialise in the gentle McTimoney chiropractic technique, offering a holistic approach to spinal health that treats your entire body as an interconnected system. This refined method uses light, precise adjustments to restore balance and alignment throughout your skeletal structure.

The McTimoney technique is particularly well-suited to women's health needs, providing effective treatment without forceful manipulation. Our approach focuses on gentle, swift movements that encourage your body's natural healing processes while addressing the root causes of discomfort.

Benefits of McTimoney Chiropractic Care

  1. Gentle whole-body adjustments that restore skeletal balance and alignment
  2. Reduced pain and improved mobility through precise, light-touch techniques
  3. Enhanced nervous system function supporting overall health and wellbeing
  4. Suitable for all ages and particularly beneficial during pregnancy and postnatal recovery

Your First Chiropractic Consultation

Your initial appointment includes a thorough assessment of your spinal health, posture, and movement patterns. We'll discuss your health history, current concerns, and lifestyle factors that may be affecting your wellbeing. This comprehensive evaluation typically takes 45 minutes and forms the foundation of your personalised treatment plan.

Conditions We Commonly Treat

  • Back pain, neck pain, and headaches through gentle spinal realignment
  • Posture-related issues from desk work and daily activities
  • Pregnancy-related discomfort and postnatal recovery support
  • Joint stiffness and mobility restrictions affecting daily life
Why Us

Why Choose McTimoney Chiropractic at Spine and Core Synergy?

Our chiropractic services integrate seamlessly with our physiotherapy and Pilates programs, creating a comprehensive approach to your spinal health. The gentle nature of the McTimoney technique makes it an ideal choice for women seeking effective treatment without aggressive manipulation, supporting your journey to confident, pain-free movement.
