Privacy Policy for Spine and Core Synergy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

At Spine and Core Synergy, we are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services or visit our website.

We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Data Protection Act 2018. This policy was last updated on [Date] and may be revised from time to time.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal details including name, address, phone number, and email address
  2. Medical history and health information relevant to your treatment

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely to provide you with the best possible care. This includes scheduling appointments, maintaining treatment records, processing payments, and communicating with you about your health and wellness journey.

Data Security and Storage

  • Access to your information is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We retain records in accordance with professional healthcare guidelines
  • Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. You may also withdraw consent for data processing at any time. To exercise these rights or if you have any privacy concerns, please contact us at [contact information].
